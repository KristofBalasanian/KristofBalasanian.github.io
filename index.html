About

Thank you for visiting my website! I’m a 4th-year Mechanical Engineering undergrad at San Diego State University, looking for full-time roles starting in summer 2026.

I have about a year of professional experience across several internships. At Tesla, I worked on a ground-up vehicle program on the integration team. Last summer I joined Amidon Heavy Industries, a pre-seed startup building fleets of autonomous underwater and surface robots for commercial ocean-infrastructure monitoring; I had a lot of ownership and hands-on build/test.

I’m currently at Kunin, a pre-seed startup where I’m responsible for modeling, fluids analysis, and BOM/CAPEX buildout for a spiral-wound, nanofiber-based ion-exchange module for selective ionic separations. The technology will be initially deployed in the mining industry. In parallel to this I have just started my capstone project on the behalf of the US Navy - Naval Information Warfare Center Pacific where I will be working on mechanical RF‑steering using a patch antenna on a gimbal for jam‑resilient swarm communications for small UAS.

In addition to my professional engineering experience I spent 3 years on my schools FSAE EV team serving as a Senior Engineer and the Vice President managing a six figure budget. I also particpated in my schools startup accelerator and was an investor for a So-Cal based angel syndicate called Crater Ventures.

Outside of everything I have mentioned, I am into triathlon, skiing, sailing, travel, a lot of good food, and going to the beach with my friends!
