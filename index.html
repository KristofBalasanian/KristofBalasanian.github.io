About

Thank you for visiting my website! I’m a 4th-year Mechanical Engineering undergrad at San Diego State University, looking for full-time roles starting in summer 2026.

I have about a year of professional experience across several internships. At Tesla, I worked on a ground-up vehicle program on the integration team, which gave me solid exposure to high-volume production. Last summer I joined a pre-seed startup (Amidon Heavy Industries) building fleets of autonomous underwater and surface robots for commercial ocean-infrastructure monitoring; I had a lot of ownership and hands-on build/test. I’m currently at another startup (Stealth) where I’m responsible for modeling, fluids analysis, and BOM/CAPEX buildout for a spiral-wound, nanofiber-based ion-exchange module for selective ionic separations.

One-liner: I turn messy constraints into clean, testable hardware—from CAD to bench to build.

Outside of building cool stuff I’m into triathlon, skiing, sailing, travel, a lot of good food, and going to the beach with my friends!
